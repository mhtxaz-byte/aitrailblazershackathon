# ApexCoach — Go-To-Market & Investor Strategy

## Executive Summary

ApexCoach is the first AI-powered Assistant Coach designed to optimize lineup decisions in real time, increasing win probability and unlocking measurable revenue growth for NCAA basketball programs.

College basketball programs operate as multi-million-dollar enterprises. Yet rotation decisions still rely heavily on instinct, fragmented analytics, and post-game analysis. ApexCoach transforms in-game, pre-game, and post-game decisions into predictive, revenue-aligned optimization.

**Initial Proof-of-Concept:** University of Arizona — McKale Center (15,000 capacity)

**Revenue Model:** 2% of incremental ticket revenue attributable to additional wins.

**Funding Ask:** $2M angel round to scale engineering, data science, and pilot expansion.

ApexCoach is positioned to become the standard AI decision layer across NCAA basketball.

## Financial Upside Model (McKale Example)

**Venue:** McKale Center, University of Arizona
- Capacity: 15,000
- Avg premium ticket: $300
- 18 home games per season
- Max ticket revenue per sold-out season: **$81M**

### Conservative Win Impact Model

Assume ApexCoach contributes to:
- +2 additional home wins
- +5% attendance lift in remaining games

**5% attendance lift per game:**
750 extra seats × $300 × 16 games = **$3.6M**

**+2 additional wins increasing demand:**
Conservative impact = **$2M incremental revenue**

**Total incremental revenue ≈ $5.6M**
**2% revenue share = $112,000 annually from one program**

This excludes: postseason revenue impact, merchandise lift, alumni engagement, media exposure value.
